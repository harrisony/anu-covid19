COVID-safe campus alert system

COVID-safe campus alert system

  • » Indicative alert levels for remote campuses
Ask a question

Current alert level

Our current campus alert level is orange - HIGH risk.

Masks

Wearing of masks is mandatory on campus at this time.

The COVID-safe campus alert system has been established to clarify ANU control practices to be activated as the COVID-19 situation changes in Australia, the ACT and other jurisdictions as appropriate. This will ensure the University will continue to operate in a COVID-safe manner despite changing circumstances.

A detailed list of changes to control measures at different risk levels is provided below. These measures are the minimum required. The local risk assessment should inform any additional measures that may be required.

Any movement within these risk levels will be determined by community transmission in the ACT or the risk of community transmission on campus. You will be alerted immediately via the ANUOK app when there is a change in our level.
